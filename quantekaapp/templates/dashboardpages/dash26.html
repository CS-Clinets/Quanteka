A safety dashboard is a set of visual reports that highlight critical safety data and key performance indicators (KPIs) in real time. Safety dashboards assist you in quickly visualising and drilling down on trends and patterns in your data to answer business concerns. Injury rate, days lost and restricted, incident severity, incident expenditures, and completion of corrective actions are all common measures.
